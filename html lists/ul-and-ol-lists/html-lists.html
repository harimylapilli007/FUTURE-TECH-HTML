Favorite places In Hyderabad

Charminar
An iconic monument and mosque, Charminar is a symbol of Hyderabad. It was built in 1591 and is located in the heart of the old city.
Salar Jung Museum
One of the largest museums in India, Salar Jung Museum houses an extensive collection of art, artifacts, and antiques from different civilizations.
Birla Mandir
Perched on a hill, Birla Mandir is a Hindu temple made of white marble and offers a panoramic view of the city.
Lumbini Park
Located along the Hussain Sagar Lake, Lumbini Park is a popular destination for leisure, with boating facilities and beautiful views.
